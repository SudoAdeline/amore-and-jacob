Adeline Amore

From classical pianist to vocalist, music has always been in her blood. Adeline Amore made her jazz singing debut in Heidelberg in 2024 and has since established herself with angelic, soothing vocals that belong to another era. A captivating performer who channels the spirit of the great jazz vocalists, she weaves through standards and original compositions with a presence that silences rooms and stirs souls.

Pierre Jacob

A jazz pianist of rare sensitivity and precision, Pierre Jacob is a regular house pianist at Cave 54 in Heidelberg — Germany's oldest jazz cellar, where legends like Louis Armstrong and Ella Fitzgerald once jammed. He brings warmth and depth to every note, his playing both technically brilliant and deeply emotional — the perfect counterpart to Adeline's voice, creating the dialogue that defines the duo's signature sound.
